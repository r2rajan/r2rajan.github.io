# Ramesh Rajan

I'm a cloud security architect and engineering leader based in the San Francisco Bay Area with over 20 years of experience in enterprise infrastructure, cloud platforms, cybersecurity, and AI.

## Career Journey

My career has evolved through several transformative phases:

- **Unix Engineer**: I started my career with a foundation focused on resilience and uptime, building the fundamentals of reliable systems.
- **Storage Engineer/Architect**: I managed multi-petabyte infrastructures at companies like eBay and PayPal, tackling challenges at massive scale.
- **Leadership at Hitachi Data Systems**: I guided global engineering teams and drove modernization initiatives across the organization.
- **Current Role**: As a Senior Security Architect at AWS, I specialize in cloud architecture and security across AWS, Azure, and GCP, working with Fortune 500 companies to design and implement secure, scalable solutions.

## Core Expertise

Throughout my career, I've maintained a **security-first mindset**, applying it to:
- Cloud migrations
- Zero-trust security models
- Infrastructure-as-Code frameworks
- Multi-cloud architectures

## Current Focus: The Intersection of AI and Security

Three key intersection points drive my work today:

1. **Security of AI systems** against adversarial threats
2. **AI applications** for detection and environment hardening
3. **Mitigating unintended consequences** of autonomous systems

I believe the future of technology lies at the convergence of cloud, security, and AI, and I'm passionate about exploring how these domains can work together to create more resilient and intelligent systems.
